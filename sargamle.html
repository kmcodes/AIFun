SARGAMLE

Listen to the hidden 5-swara tune, then guess it on the pads — you get 6 tries. Green = right swara, right beat. Amber = swara is in the tune, different beat. Replay the tune as often as you like; the tanpura drone holds Sa under everything so your ear has an anchor.

Made as an independent ear-training experiment.

Sargamle uses optional analytics to understand how many people play and which game features are useful. Analytics is off unless you accept. The game works either way.

Optional Google Analytics
